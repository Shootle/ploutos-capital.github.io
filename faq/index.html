LUNA

What is LUNA?

LUNA is a private, diversified crypto fund designed to mitigate losses in particular crypto markets while taking advantage of overall crypto market growth and arbitrage opportunities. It is a private fund set up between friends.

What are LUNA tokens?

LUNA tokens are an ERC20 token that represents a profit share stake between friends in the LUNA Fund.

How many LUNA tokens are there?

LIVE TOKENS  27,874.44024

What are “LIVE” LUNA tokens?

“LIVE” LUNA tokens are all the tokens in circulation, held by the Team and Investor friends. They are the only LUNA tokens that hold value.

What are “FROZEN” LUNA tokens?

“FROZEN” LUNA tokens are all the tokens outside of circulation, held in a frozen account awaiting the monthly dividend and drip cycle.

When will dividends be paid out?

The dividend period will be on or closely around the 1st of every month.

When is the first dividend payout?

The first dividend payout will happen on or around the 1st of February, 2018.

Can I send my LUNA tokens to someone else?

Yes, you can send LUNA tokens to anyone so long as it’s an ERC20 compatible wallet.

Can I receive dividends in anything else than LUNA?

Yes, you’ll have the choice to exchange your dividend LUNA for an equivalent value of ETH should you prefer.

Can I buy LUNA tokens once the pICO is finished?

Once the pICO is finished, you’ll need to wait to the Drip period once a month for the opportunity to buy any more LUNA. See the Token Drip section in the LUNA documentation.

Can I sell my LUNA tokens for ETH?

You can sell LUNA dividend tokens for ETH directly to the company during the monthly buyback period. Also, you may arrange with other investors to purchase your tokens.

How can I follow updates on the fund?

You can follow the fund’s progress on this site, on Medium, on Steemit, and by joining our LUNA fund investors group on Telegram.

LUNA pICO

What is a pICO?

A pICO is a Private Initial Coin Offering. It is simply a private contract between friends - in our case, an ETH smart contract.

When does the LUNA pICO begin?

The pICO begins on December 19th, 2017 at 3:00pm GMT.

When does the LUNA pICO end?

The pICO ends on December 21st, 2017 at 6:00am GMT.

What is the pICO hardcap?

The pICO hardcap is 40 Ether.
