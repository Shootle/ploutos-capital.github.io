LUNA Fund pICO

LUNA is a private, diversified crypto fund designed to mitigate losses in particular crypto markets while taking advantage of overall crypto market growth and arbitrage opportunities.

LUNA is using a new dividend model that aims to tackle some of the major issues that current smart contract based funds are facing. It is our hope to not only run a highly successful fund but to also create a proof of concept dividend model to help solve problems that exist in current token based models. LUNA looks to add smart contract value through:

  • Ability for people to reinvest their profits back into the fund
  • Ability for a fund to grow outside of its initial offering plus subsequent profits minus dividends
  • Ability for a fund to serve two different mindsets of investors in tandem:
    • Those that only want monthly/quarterly dividends
    • Those that want to reinvest in the fund for longer term fund health/growth.
